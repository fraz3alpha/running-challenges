Version 0.7.4 Released

Dec 3, 2018

It’s Christmas!

Well, it’s Christmas soon, and to celebrate, we have released a new running badge to adorn those runners who have managed to run on Christmas day.

(We’d really like to give extra special kudos to those volunteers giving up their Christmas morning, but until the parkrun website lists the dates of each volunteer role, unfortunately we aren’t able to do so.)

Additionally, we have added the ‘All Weather Runner’ badge, contributed by Russell Boyatt - this badge celebrates those parkrunners who have kept up their attendence come rain or shime and have run at least once in every calendar month (they don’t have to be in the same year).

Happy parkrunning!
